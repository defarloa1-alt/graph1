ContextThe graph currently holds 5,218 confirmable persons spread across two namespaces. 4,772 were ingested directly from the Digital Prosopography of the Roman Republic (DPRR) with a person_ entity_id prefix. A further 382 Wikidata-confirmed humans exist in the same bucket, and 64 persons leaked into the concept_ namespace during harvest of downstream relations. No :Person label exists yet; persons are identified solely by entity_type='PERSON' or entity_id prefix.The DPRR label string encodes the full tria nomina, filiation, tribal affiliation, and cognomen in a structured format (e.g., POMP1976 Cn. Pompeius (31) Cn. f. Sex. n. Clu. Magnus) — onomastic data currently locked in a string and unavailable for graph queries.Existing P-code relationships (P21, P27, P19, P20, P106, P1343) are not yet mapped to canonical Chrystallum relationship types. First-class onomastic node types (Gens, Praenomen, Cognomen, Tribe, Polity) do not exist. This ADR defines both.2. :Person Label Application RulesThe :Person label is applied as a dual-label alongside :Entity. It is an ontological assertion, not an ingestion artifact, and must satisfy at least one confirmation gate while passing the veto check.Gate conditions (any one sufficient)GateConditionPopulationRationaleAn.dprr_id IS NOT NULL4,772DPRR is curated scholarly prosopography — primary source authorityB(n)-[:P31]->({label:'human'}) AND entity_id STARTS WITH 'person_'382Wikidata-confirmed human in correct ingest namespaceC(n)-[:P31]->({label:'human'}) AND entity_type = 'CONCEPT'64Namespace leak — correct entity_type at same time as labellingVeto condition (suppresses :Person regardless of gate)EXISTS { (n)-[:P31]->(m:Entity) WHERE m.label <> 'human' AND n.dprr_id IS NULL }Covers 25 nodes with person_ prefix whose P31 resolves to non-human entities (places, abstract concepts, groups). It is too high for ancient persons where even primary sources do not support 0.9 confidence on birth years and office dates.A domain-scoped threshold override should be introduced:claim_promotion_confidence_ancient_person = 0.75Applied when the Person node has an IN_PERIOD edge to a Periodo_Period with end_date before year 0. This calibrates the threshold to what the historical evidence can actually support, rather than silently blocking half the ancient person corpus from ever promoting.This threshold should be registered as a new SYS_Threshold node with decision_table = D10_DETERMINE_claim_promotion_eligibility and a domain_scope property of 'ancient_person'.8.
